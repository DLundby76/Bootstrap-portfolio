About Me

Daniel Lundby is an American fiber artist and award winning quilter. Born and raised in Iowa, Daniel went to catholic school most of this life, but ended up at Iowa State University for both his undergrad and gradute degrees in Apparel Design, Manufacturing and Production. While most of his professioal career Daniel resided in New York City's Mott Haven section of the Bronx. It wasn't until recently, that Daniel moved to the city of Denver Colorado to persue his passion for art and design.

Current Daniel has focused his creative efforts on the creation of graphic quilts using vintage textiles sourced from old bedsheets from the 70's and 80's. His most popular quilts feature textiles with art from the original 3 Star Warm Movies. Celebrity chef and Denver restaurateur Justin Brunson was one of the first to purchase a Star Wars quilt from artist Daniel Lundby
